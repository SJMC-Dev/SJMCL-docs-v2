# User Group

All Minecraft players are welcome to join the SJMCL user community. You can discuss launcher usage, user experience, features, and related topics with other users, and you can also directly raise launcher-related questions, suggestions, or feedback in the group. The developers will respond and follow up when they see them.

Discussion about Minecraft gameplay and related topics is also welcome. Feel free to speak openly, but please pay attention to the following group chat rules:

1. Maintain a kind and friendly attitude, and communicate with other group members about technology, user experience, gameplay, and related topics.
2. Do not engage in personal attacks, flame wars, ridicule, passive-aggressive behavior, or similar conduct in the group.
3. Do not post sexual content, political content, advertisements unrelated to Minecraft, or any otherwise inappropriate content in the group. Non-profit servers, modpacks, and similar content may be shared appropriately.
4. Please pay close attention to your personal privacy and information security.
