# Icons

Icons play the core part in SJMC Launcher's visual identity.

When designing content related to the SJMC Launcher, it is recommended that you use official icons to ensure consistency.

## Icon Interpretation

The standard SJMC Launcher icon consists of a central main pattern, a silhouette of Shanghai Jiao Tong University’s East Gate and a gradient hexagonal background.

Drawing on design elements such as books, anvil and gear from SJTU's emblem, the main pattern incorporates the iconic Creeper face in Minecraft.
The East Gate silhouette further emphasizes SJMCL'S SJTU identity.

As a whole, the icon represents the development team’s foundation in the spirit of SJTU. It carries the SJTU Minecraft Club’s original aspiration of loving MC and loving SJTU even more, together with passion for technical development.
On this basis, it expresses our sincere intention to create a practical and energetic launcher for the community.
